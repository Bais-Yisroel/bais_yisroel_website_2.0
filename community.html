About: Congregation Bais Yisroel

Our shul provides an opportunity for every Jew to grow closer to Hashem (G-D) and His Torah. We emphasize the study of Torah and live according to its guidelines and teachings. This wisdom adds a new dimension of success, understanding, and purpose to each moment and every detail of life. Bais Yisroel offers boys' and girls' Orthodox youth groups for grades 1-8.
